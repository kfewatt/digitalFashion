DIGITAL FASHION

The uprising of clothes with no fabric

Social media's impact on how we consume clothing has had a devastating effect on the environment. Digital fashion presents a solution; instead of actually creating and sewing a garment that will be photographed once and then thrown aside, why not skip the manufactoring process entirely? A new service where purchasing clothing means having its image photshopped onto yours is sweeping the fashion industry.
